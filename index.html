Bible Gateway Dark Mode

Read the Bible more comfortably.

Download from Chrome Store

Why Dark Mode?

Reading long passages on a bright white screen can strain your eyes. This extension brings a clean, dark theme to BibleGateway.com, perfect for night reading or long study sessions.

Features

  • One-click toggle for dark mode
  • Darker than other dark modes
  • Lightweight and free. Donate if you like it.

How to Use

  1. Download from Chrome Store
  2. Open BibleGateway.com.
  3. Enjoy comfortable reading.
