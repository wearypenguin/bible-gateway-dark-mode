Privacy Policy

Bible Gateway Dark Mode does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The extension runs entirely in your browser and only changes the appearance of BibleGateway.com pages by applying dark-themed styles.

We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking of any kind. Any permissions requested are strictly for enabling dark mode functionality on BibleGateway.com.
